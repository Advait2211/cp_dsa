Product 04

Our Solution

Jan-Seva
A WhatsApp-based employment and service discovery platform for India's 500 million low-digital-literacy users. No app downloads. No English required. No expensive commissions. Just a WhatsApp message to connect workers with the customers who need them — in their language, in their neighbourhood.

Business 06

Unit Economics

₹5

per user per month — affordable enough to scale across millions of rural users through government and NGO partnerships.

WhatsApp API (50 msgs/req × 10 req) ₹3.00
AI Processing — STT (10–15 sec avg) ₹1.00
LLM — Intent + Language + Matching ₹1.00
Database & Server (per 1000 users) ₹0.50
Total Cost / User / Month ₹5.00

Why This Model Works

Government Subsidy Model
At ₹5/user, state governments can subsidize entire districts as part of livelihood schemes. Cost is lower than a single SMS bulk campaign.
NGO Partnership
NGOs working in rural employment can onboard entire communities for less than ₹500/month per 100 users.
Worker Subscription
Workers pay ₹20–₹50/month — a fraction of what they'd earn from a single additional job per month found through the platform.
